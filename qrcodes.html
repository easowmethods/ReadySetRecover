QR Code Hunt Cards — Print & Post Around the Room

Before the session:
1. Open this page with internet access so the QR codes generate below.
2. Click PRINT QR CARDS (or press Ctrl+P / Cmd+P).
3. Cut out the five cards and post them around the room — on walls, pillars, doors, or table stands.
4. Space them out so players have to physically move between them. The hunt is the point.

Order matters: Players scan QR Code #1 first, then #2, then #3, and so on. Post them in a logical route around the room.

Both crisis scenarios use the same 5 QR codes. The spin wheel determines which set of questions appears — the QR codes are identical for both levels.
